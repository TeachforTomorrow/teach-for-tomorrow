About Us

Education is a gift; sadly, one that often comes with a price tag. We’re here to change that. Our group of dedicated volunteers are matched with students, and are there for them as an educational resource.

We provide individualized tutoring and homework help at absolutely no cost: each tutor works with just one student at a time, ensuring they receive enough support to be able to ask questions at any time, as well as become confident in their abilities.

Join Us!

Whether it be online or in person, our tutors are committed to bringing the best possible experience to students. Though our primary purpose is to help students with their classwork, personal connections are just as important as academic success. Our goal is to not only help students learn the material, but help them stay engaged and interested.

This story, though, is far from over. Though we have a team of around fifteen tutors, we can always use more helping hands. Sign up today to be a part of something meaningful, and contact us if you’re interested in setting up a chapter at your school!
